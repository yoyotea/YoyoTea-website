Before You Book

Venue
Considerations

Space needed: The booth requires a 3m × 3m area. Please ensure your venue can accommodate this footprint.

Height clearance: A minimum of 2.7m height clearance is required for backdrop installation.

Power access: We'll need access to a nearby power port. Please check this with your venue in advance.

Indoor / undercover: We require a flat, level floor and indoor or undercover placement for optimal lighting and photo quality.

Parking & loading: Please obtain loading zone and parking information from your venue prior to your event.

Stairs or lifts: Please notify us in advance if your venue has stairs or small elevators so we can plan accordingly.

Good to Know

Important Info

Allergen Information

Our ingredients are sourced from various suppliers and may be processed in facilities handling common allergens including soy, egg, and dairy. We take precautions to minimise cross-contamination but cannot guarantee complete absence. Milk alternatives (soy, oat, almond) are available but may contain allergens specific to their source.

Please inform us of any allergies or dietary needs — we'll do our best to accommodate.
